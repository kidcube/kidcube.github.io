KidCube’s premium features are purchased online or via Apple’s iTunes store. When you purchase these features, you are also subject to the iTunes terms of service.

Payment plan

The basic version of KidCube is available for free. To access the premium features of KidCube, which has additional features, including (but not limited to) goals & personalised training, access to all games, access to advanced statistics, comparisons with other users and early access to new games, you will have to pay via an auto-renewing subscription from Apple’s iTunes store.

Renewals and cancellations

KidCube is purchased via an iTunes auto-renewing subscription. This means your subscription is automatically renewed at the end of the subscription period.

You can modify or cancel your subscription at any time from within the application (if purchased online) or by signing in to your iTunes account on your computer or iOS or Android device and changing the settings.

iOS: http://support.apple.com/kb/HT4098
